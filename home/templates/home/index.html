WHO WE ARE:
Sloan Business Club (SBC) is MIT's largest undergraduate business organization.

SBC was founded on the belief that while MIT provides its students with an excellent academic and intellectual foundation, the successful business person requires opportunities and skills that cannot be bestowed in a classroom setting.
OUR MISSION:
Through our extensive selection of events, our organization works to provide our 1,600+ members with both an expansive business network and a solid mentorship base, enabling students to fully utilize their academic backgrounds in launching strong businesses.

Our members have broad interests, ranging from trading and banking to consulting and engineering management. We strive to develop students' skills and prepare them for the workplace.
